Calzone

So let's be real here, a calzone (calsoNEH) is basically just a pizza that's been stuffed and folded over, but incredibly delicious if done the correct way. In my humble opinion, the best version of a calzone and how to make it is a little char, nice and puffy, big air pockets, and sauce on top AFTER it's baked.

Preparation Time

30 mins

Cook Time

30 mins

Servings

2 servings

Instructions

Step 1

In a large bowl, add in flour and sea salt and mix together. Mix together 95F water with instant yeast. Once mixed and dissolved, pour the water into the flour mixture.

Step 2

Mix by hand until a shaggy dough forms. Dump onto a work surface and knead lightly for 1-2 minutes. Place in a bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Let rise at room temperature for 18-24 hours.

Step 3

On a lightly floured surface, dump out the dough and divide into 4 equal pieces. Stretch and pull sides over itself and roll over seam side down rolling into a taut ball. Place dough balls onto a flour dusted baking sheet and cover with an inverted baking sheet. Let rest for at room temperature for 4 hours.

Step 4

Set a pizza stone in a 500F oven and let preheat for 45 minutes prior to baking. Press a dough ball down into a disc then stretch/roll out to a larger disc. On the bottom half, fill with meat and cheese fillings, peppers and onions, more meat and cheese.

Step 5

Drape the top layer of dough over the other half and pinch together. Once pinched, fold the edge back over itself pressing again to create a double seal. Brush the top with olive oil and a small amount of grated cheese.

Step 6

Bake onto the pizza oven and bake for 8-12 minutes. Remove and top with sauce, grated cheese, and basil before serving.

Step 7

In a medium saucepan, add olive oil and heat over medium heat. Add chopped garlic once hot and let cook for 1-2 minutes. Add crushed red chili flakes and toast for 20 seconds before adding crushed San Marzano tomatoes. Add pinch of sugar and bring to a simmer.

Step 8

Reduce to low and let sauce reduce for 8 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 9

In a medium saute pan, add oil and heat over medium heat until shimmering. Add in peppers and onions. Season with salt and lightly toss. Saute for 4-5 minutes.

Ingredients

700 g Double OO Tipo Flour

14 g Fine sea salt

410 g Filtered Water

0.75 g Instant yeast

6.5 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil

4 cloves garlic, roughly chopped

1 tsp crushed red chili flakes

28 oz can crushed San Marzano Tomatoes

Sugar

Salt and pepper to taste

1 large sweet onion, thinly sliced

Genoa Salami

2 cups Mozzarella, shredded

1/2 cup Parmigiano Reggiano, shredded
