Bibimbap

Homemade bibimbap might look fancy, but it's actually relatively easy to do. You can add or remove as many ingredients as you want to make it the perfect bibimbap for you.

Preparation Time

30 mins

Cook Time

30 mins

Servings

2 servings

Instructions

Step 1

Pat the meat dry and cut into 1/4" slices and toss into a bowl. To the bowl, add chopped garlic, sweet soy sauce, dark soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and honey. Mix together by hand until thoroughly combined.

Step 2

Cover and refrigerate for at least 20 minutes. Once the grill is screaming hot, grill the meat in batches for 30 seconds to 1 minute on both sides.

Step 3

In a medium saucepot, add in 1/2 cup mirin, 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/2 cup white distilled vinegar 5 cloves garlic, bay leaves, 1 shallot. Set over medium heat until it just begins to steam.

Step 4

Separately, slice mushrooms into quarters and toss them into 350F oil to fry, stirring occasionally, until deep golden brown. Place mushrooms directly into the hot marinade and pour into a container and let sit until cooled completely.

Step 5

Slice the cucumber into quarters and scoring in a crosshatch pattern on both sides. Season with salt, toss together, and let cure at room temperature for 15 minutes. Drain, and rinse lightly. Add spicy chili crisp, lemon juice to taste, and grated garlic cloves. Toss to coat.

Step 6

In a medium bowl, add grated ginger, fish sauce, and gochugaru mixing until combined. Peel and julienne the carrots. Toss in the paste.

Step 7

In a large pan, add a light drizzle of sesame oil heating over medium heat. Add one bunch of fresh spinach, season to taste with salt, and let cook stirring often. Cook until wilted. Cut the hit and add chopped garlic tossing together. Add to a bowl and place to the side.

Step 8

In a medium pot, heat a pot of water to a boil. Add bean sprouts in batches and boil for 30 seconds. Immediately toss in ice water until chilled. Remove and drain on a paper towel.

Step 9

In a separate small pot, heat oil and sliced garlic over medium heat. Constantly stir. Once the garlic is golden, cut off the heat and add in the sprouts tossing to coat. Add fish sauce and stir together until combined.

Step 10

To a shallow bowl, add a generous amount of steamed rice. Add the mushrooms with a drizzle of marinade followed by spinach, bean sprouts, carrots, and cucumbers. Add on the meat and topped with a fried egg.

Step 11

Finish with a drizzle of sauce.

Ingredients

5 tablespoons gochujang

2 cups mirin

4 tablespoons rice vinegar

2 tablespoon honey

3 teaspoons dark soy sauce

1.5 tbsp grated ginger

5 teaspoons toasted sesame oil

1/2 lb boneless short rib

1 thick ribeye steak

17 cloves garlic

1/4 cup sweet soy

1 seedless english cucumber

Salt and pepper to taste

2 tablespoons spicy chili crisp

Lemon juice

1lb Baby king oyster mushrooms

1/2 cup soy sauce

1/2 cup white distilled vinegar

2 bay leaves

1 shallot quartered

1 bunch fresh spinach

2lb carrots

1 tablespoon gochugaru

1/4 cup thinly sliced green onion

1 tablespoon fish sauce

2 cups bean sprouts

Eggs
