French Toast

French toast is a classic breakfast dish that is loved by many. It is made by soaking slices of bread in a mixture of beaten eggs, milk, and vanilla extract, and then cooking them on a griddle or in a frying pan until they are golden brown and crispy on the outside.

Preparation Time

10 mins

Cook Time

7 mins

Servings

4 servings

Instructions

Step 1

In a medium-size bowl, whisk together the eggs and egg yolks along with ground cinnamon, fine sea salt, light brown sugar, and grated nutmeg.

Step 2

Once combined, whisk in heavy cream and whole milk. Pour into a dish or container.

Step 3

Slice the desired bread. Dunk the slices into the custard and sit for a few seconds.

Step 4

In a large pan greased with melted unsalted butter, lay in two slices of bread and cook/toast for 3 minutes. Flip and repeat.

Step 5

Place onto a plate, top with a small piece of butter, and drizzle with maple syrup.

Ingredients

3 eggs

2 egg yolks

1 tsp ground cinnamon

1/2 tsp fine sea salt

1.5 Tbs light brown sugar

Pinch freshly grated nutmeg

1/2 cup heavy whipping cream

3/4 cup whole milk
