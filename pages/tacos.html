Breakfast Tacos

Breakfast tacos are a delicious and easy-to-make breakfast dish that are perfect for those on-the-go mornings. Soft tortillas are filled with scrambled eggs and a variety of savory toppings such as crispy bacon, spicy chorizo, or creamy avocado. These tacos can be customized to suit any taste or dietary preference, and are a great way to start the day with a satisfying and protein-packed meal. Whether you're a fan of classic Tex-Mex flavors or prefer to mix things up with your own unique combination of ingredients, breakfast tacos are a versatile and flavorful way to fuel your day.

Preparation Time

30 mins

Cook Time

30 mins

Servings

2 servings

Instructions

Step 1

Whisk and combine all-purpose flour with baking powder and kosher salt.

Step 2

Add in lard mix into the flour using two forks.

Step 3

Stirring with a fork slowly stream in warm water.

Step 4

Gently knead into a ball and stop immediately. Place into a greased bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.

Step 5

On a lightly floured surface, dump out the dough and divide into equal pieces and roll into a ball. Cover and let rest for 5 minutes.

Step 6

Heat a cast iron skillet of medium-high heat. On a generously floured surface, dust the dough ball then roll and rotate in even circles until ⅛” thick. Lay into a hot cast iron skillet and cook 30 seconds per side.

Step 7

Place tortillas on a plate and cover with a towel to keep warm.

Step 8

Start by frying or baking the bacon until crispy. Drain on paper towels.

Step 9

Melt butter over medium heat in a medium skillet. Beat the eggs together and add to the pan once the butter is melted. Stir and cook over medium heat until soft scramble forms.

Step 10

Take a tortilla and lay a base of scrambled eggs on followed by shredded cheese and top with bacon.

Ingredients

2.5 cups all purpose flour

1/2 cup lard or shortening

1.5 tsp. baking powder

2 tsp kosher salt

2/3 cup hot water

1 1/2 tbsp oil

1 tbsp unsalted butter

1/2 onion

11 cloves garlic

1 sliced serrano

1 can black beans

Salt and pepper to taste

1.25 lb jalapenos

Juice of 1 lime
